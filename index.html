Why Move to the Edge?

Shift from "Margin Destruction" to sustainable scaling.

Controllable Unit Economics

Stop the variable "Cloud Burn." Replace unpredictable $1.50/user variable costs with a fixed $0.15/device fee. By moving inference to the edge, you slash infrastructure bills by up to 90%.

No Testing Punishment

Scaling your testing shouldn't break your bank. Because edge pricing is fixed per device, your gross margins remain at a healthy 93.3% to 94.4% regardless of how many tokens your users generate.

Intelligent Hybrid RAG

Route to cloud only when it makes sense. Keep 90% of interactions local for privacy and speed, while utilizing cloud-based Vector DBs only for complex retrievals.

Dual Deployment Architectures

SlyOS isn't just for phones. It's an infrastructure layer that adapts to the available compute environment.

Architecture A

Direct Edge Inference

The standard SlyOS flow. A user's single device downloads an optimized SLM (e.g., 360M params) and runs it completely independently.

  • > Ultra-low latency
  • > Works offline
  • > 100% Privacy

Technical FAQ

The SDK runs a hardware check SlyOS.checkCompatibility() before initializing. If the device lacks WebGPU or sufficient RAM, you can configure a fallback to our cloud API.
Inference is energy-intensive, but we use optimized WebGPU shaders significantly more efficient than CPU inference. For short tasks the impact is negligible.
